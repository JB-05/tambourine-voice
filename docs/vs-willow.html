Tambourine vs Willow Voice

Both offer AI-powered voice dictation. Here's how they differ.

Quick Summary

Willow Voice is a VC-backed, polished voice dictation service with enterprise customers and a focus on accuracy. Tambourine is an open-source alternative that gives you full control: self-host on your infrastructure, choose from 10+ STT/LLM providers, customize formatting prompts, or run fully local with Whisper + Ollama.
